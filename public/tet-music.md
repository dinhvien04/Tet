# Tết Background Music

For the video recap feature, you'll need to add a Tết-themed background music file.

## Instructions:

1. Find a royalty-free Tết music track (instrumental version recommended)
2. Convert it to MP3 format
3. Save it as `public/tet-music.mp3`

## Recommended sources for royalty-free music:
- YouTube Audio Library
- Free Music Archive
- Incompetech
- Bensound

## Music characteristics:
- Duration: 30-60 seconds (will loop if needed)
- Format: MP3
- Bitrate: 128-192 kbps
- Style: Traditional Vietnamese Tết music (instrumental)

## Note:
The video creation will work without music, but adding music enhances the experience.
